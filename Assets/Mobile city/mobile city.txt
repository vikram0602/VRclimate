This package’s intention is to provide the base for building a city for mobile. Low poly, batching, LODs ( Level Of Detail ) and low texture use were the main concept for the making.


_____________________________________
How to use:

Check the included Mobile_City_reference_guide.pdf file or http://tinyurl.com/mobilecity for a more updated guide.
